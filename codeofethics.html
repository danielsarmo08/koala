Koala T-shirt Code of Ethics

At Koala T-shirt, we are committed to honesty, fairness, and respect in everything we do. Our Code of Ethics guides how we treat our customers, staff, and partners.
a. User Information We respect your privacy and only collect information needed to provide our services. We keep your details confidential and secure.
b. Access and Equity We believe everyone deserves equal access to our products and services. We do not discriminate and welcome all customers and staff, regardless of background.
c. Fair Practices We commit to fair pricing, honest advertising, and respectful communication. We aim to provide quality products and excellent customer service.
d. Grievance Procedure If you have any complaints or concerns, please contact us immediately. We will listen carefully and work to resolve issues fairly and quickly. You can find our grievance form on the koala.com.au
