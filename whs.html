Our WHS Commitments

We follow all workplace health and safety laws and regulations to prevent accidents and injuries.
We provide a clean, organized, and hazard-free environment in the store and office areas.
We regularly train our staff on safe work practices and emergency procedures.
We encourage all employees to report any safety concerns or hazards immediately.
We maintain proper equipment and tools to ensure they are safe to use.
We have clear procedures to respond to injuries or emergencies quickly and effectively.
Responsibilities Management is responsible for enforcing safety policies and ensuring all risks are minimized.
Staff must follow safety guidelines, use equipment properly, and report hazards or incidents.
Everyone should work together to create a safe and supportive workplace.
By working together and staying aware, Koala T-shirt aims to keep everyone safe and healthy every day.
